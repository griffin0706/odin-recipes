Filipino Fried Chicken

Ingredients for Filipino Fried Chicken

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 2 cups cooking oil
  • 2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 10 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 16 grams Maggi Magic Sarap
  • 3 pieces calamansi
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika

How to Cook Filipino Fried Chicken

Let’s break this down into four easy steps:

  1. Combine the marinade ingredients in a bowl. Mix well. 16 grams Maggi Magic Sarap, 3 pieces calamansi, ½ teaspoon ground black pepper, 1 teaspoon paprika, 1/4 cup soy sauce
  2. Put the chicken in a resealable bag. Pour-in the marinade mixture. Let all the air out of the bag, seal it, and then place inside the refrigerator. Marinate for at least 1 hour. 1 whole chicken
  3. Combine the breading ingredients in a bowl. Mix well. 2 teaspoon garlic powder, 1 teaspoon onion powder, ⅛ teaspoon baking powder, 2 tablespoons cornstarch, 10 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  4. Dredge the marinated chicken in the breading mixture. Shake off the excess and place the chicken on a plate.
  5. Meanwhile, heat the cooking oil in a wok until it reaches 300 F. 2 cups cooking oil
  6. Deep fry the chicken for 25 minutes while turning the chicken over in the middle of the process.
  7. Remove the Filipino fried chicken from the wok. Place it over a wire rack to cool down.
  8. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve with Filipino Chicken Gravy.
  9. Share and Enjoy.
